◆ begin()

void Joystick::begin ( )

Initializes hardware pins and performs auto-calibration of the center point.

  1. Sets X and Y pins to INPUT mode.
  2. Sets Z (button) pin to INPUT_PULLUP to prevent floating states.
  3. Configures the Global ADC resolution to 12-bit (0-4095).
  4. Calibrates the joystick by averaging the first 10 readings at rest.
    Warning
    Ensure the joystick is untouched at the moment this is called, as it defines the "zero" point for all future movement logic.
